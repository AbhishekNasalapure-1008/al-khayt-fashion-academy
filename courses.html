Our Courses

Choose your path to fashion excellence

Sewing

Learn the art of stitching garments with precision, from hand sewing basics to mastering sewing machines for professional finishes. This comprehensive course covers fundamental techniques, fabric handling, seam finishing, and advanced construction methods. You'll gain hands-on experience with various fabrics and learn to create garments from start to finish.

Enroll in Sewing

Pattern Making

Understand garment structure by drafting accurate patterns, learning measurements, cutting techniques, and creating perfect fits for every design. Master the technical foundation of fashion design through precise pattern drafting, grading, and modification. Learn to translate design ideas into wearable garments with proper measurements and construction techniques.

Enroll in Pattern Making

Draping

Discover how fabric flows and falls by practicing draping directly on mannequins, bringing fashion ideas to life in 3D form. This hands-on course teaches you to manipulate fabric on dress forms, creating beautiful silhouettes and unique designs. Learn the art of working with different fabric types and understand how draping transforms flat materials into dimensional garments.

Enroll in Draping

Fashion Illustration

Express creativity through sketching garments, figures, and styles, learning proportions, colors, and details to visualize fashion designs beautifully. Develop your artistic voice through figure drawing, garment rendering, and color application. Learn professional techniques for presenting fashion concepts and creating compelling design portfolios.

Enroll in Fashion Illustration

Embroidery

Master decorative stitching techniques to enhance garments with intricate hand or machine embroidery, adding beauty and uniqueness to fashion creations. Learn traditional and contemporary embroidery styles, thread work, beading, and embellishment techniques. Transform plain fabrics into works of art with detailed ornamental stitching.

Enroll in Embroidery

Drawing and Painting

Build artistic skills through drawing and painting exercises, focusing on lines, shapes, color theory, and creativity for design expression. Develop a strong foundation in visual arts with techniques applicable to fashion design. Learn composition, perspective, and color mixing to enhance your creative expression and design communication.

Enroll in Drawing and Painting

Learn pencil shading techniques to create depth, texture, and realism in sketches, enhancing your ability to illustrate designs effectively.
